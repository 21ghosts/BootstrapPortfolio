About Me

I was born January 5, 1993, in Frankfurt, Germany. My parents were not in the military, though my grandparents were at the time. My mother and I soon moved back to the states after my birth. I attended school in Pooler, and graduated from Groves High School. I fancied myself keen when it came to money and decided to save by going to a two year college, walking away with two associate degrees. Now somewhere between all of that I gained a great appeciation for music, art, and coffee, along with downhill longboarding and anime.
